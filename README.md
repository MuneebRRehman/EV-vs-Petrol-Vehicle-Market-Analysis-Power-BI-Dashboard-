Project Overview

This project analyzes the global shift from traditional fuel vehicles to electric vehicles using an interactive Power BI dashboard. The goal of this project is to explore EV adoption trends, compare EV sales with petrol and diesel vehicles, and understand how factors like charging infrastructure and GDP influence EV market growth.

The dashboard provides a clear and interactive view of global vehicle market trends and helps identify key markets leading the transition toward electric mobility.

Dashboard Insights

The dashboard highlights several important aspects of the EV market:

Vehicle Sales Overview

Total vehicles sold across EV, petrol, and diesel categories

Growth indicators showing year-over-year changes in vehicle sales

EV Market Trends

EV market share growth over time

Comparison of EV, petrol, and diesel sales trends from 2010–2025

Global EV Adoption

Top 10 countries leading the EV market

Distribution of charging infrastructure across different regions

Economic Factors

Relationship between EV adoption and GDP per capita

Impact of charging infrastructure on EV sales
